About Me

Hello, I'm Zion Wu. I was born in the West Ashley neighborhood of Charleston, SC, and raised in various cities including San Francisco and Oakland, Atlanta,
Memphis, Chester, Queens, Seattle, Jacksonville, and Baton Rouge. Each of these places has played a significant role in shaping who I am today.
I’m passionate about music, dance, martial arts, and constantly learning new things. My journey began with a deep interest in movies, music videos,
and fight clips on the internet and DVDs. My grandfather inspired me to explore different dance and martial arts styles such as popping and breakdancing.
This website is a reflection of my experiences, skills, and the community I've built over time, and I hope you enjoy the content I share.
As for my family heritage, I come from a rich and diverse background. On my maternal side, I have Nigerian, Chinese, Polish, Japanese, and Native American
roots, while my paternal side brings Jamaican and Colombian heritage. I embrace and celebrate my unique background, which has greatly influenced who I am today.
